We are pioneers of Sri Lankan Safaris, Experiential Travel and  Hospitality industry in Sri Lanka, and as such, understanding the requirements of this sophisticated sector. Our mother company Eco Team Sri Lanka pioneered Tented Safari Camp concept and the Bush Walks Camp concept in Sri Lanka and currently own and operate Safari Camps in 12 impressive locations in the country.   

 

 

 

We don’t just focus on Jeep safaris but a whole array of safari activities including bush walks, canoeing, night drives, and walks, meeting the field experts, etc. Adventure and Culture are also brought in, to give balance and variety to tours.  We are very keen to change the way people perceive Wildlife in Sri Lanka, taking guests into the more remote and therefore less visited parks - without constantly doing the popular and more frequented parks only.
